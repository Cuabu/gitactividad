About

Stranger Things is an American science fiction horror television series created by the Duffer Brothers. The show is set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana and focuses on a group of kids and their encounters with supernatural forces.

The first season premiered on Netflix on July 15, 2016, and quickly became a cultural phenomenon, praised for its nostalgic references to 1980s pop culture and its compelling storytelling.

Stranger Things has received numerous awards and nominations, including several Emmy Awards. It has also spawned a wide range of merchandise and has inspired a dedicated fanbase.

Characters

Eleven

Eleven, also known as El, is a young girl with psychokinetic abilities. She escapes from a secretive government laboratory and befriends a group of boys in Hawkins.
